Privacy Policy


SCOPE OF POOLPAY PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to PoolPay's Privacy Statement! PoolPay prioritizes user online privacy and security. This privacy policy covers a variety of facets, including data collection, protection, disclosure, retention, and use of user information at our disposal. This Privacy Policy applies to the PoolPay website, and mobile application and will also include any future projects that AV Ltd. may endeavor into. The Privacy policy addresses the personal information users provide or obtain as a result of using our services and how we use user information in connection with those services.

By registering and signing up for PoolPay, users agree to our Privacy Statement. If any user has any discrepancy with either the privacy policy or any of the terms and conditions, the user may approach our support team at ranjini@poolpay.



DATA COLLECTED

i.	Registration Information
When a user signs up to create an account with PoolPay, they shall be asked to provide registration information such as the user’s name, email address, and the user phone number. PoolPay requires that the user provide their email address to avail of the PoolPay services so PoolPay can contact the user about their account and access their data from any device and location. The user is required to provide their full name for a PoolPay user ID which other users can use to add to a given pool. PoolPay also asks for the users’ phone numbers, which serves as a means to collect  any OTPs that may be required and a way for users to find each other. Furthermore, providing a phone number offers a second layer of protection. 

ii.	Personal Information
The personal data that PoolPay collects directly or indirectly within the app shall include the following but will not be limited to: 
a)	Full Name;
b)	Aadhar Card;
c)	Pan Card;
d)	Email Addresses;
e)	Financial data: if applicable, financial data that is required and/or convenient to comply with the Company's duties arising from the legal relationship between the Company and the user, such as Bank Name, Account Number and Type, CRN, beneficiary names, etc; and also extend UPI under its ambit.

The information the user provides must be accurate and complete. As a result, the onus is on the users to submit authentic data, and PoolPay will bear no responsibility in this respect.

iii.	Device and Location Information
We collect information about a user’s device and location when they register on PoolPay, thereby allowing us to provide our users better service with increased account security. It also allows us to provide location-based services such as customized advertising, search results, and other tailored content. We also collect information about user interactions with PoolPay, such as users’ device browser type, recently viewed pages, advertising clicked on, average time spent on our Sites , users’ IP address, unique device identifiers, operating system version, and app version, and users’ IP address, unique device identifiers, operating system version, and app versions.
 
iv.	E-Mail Accounts
When a user provides their e-mail address to create a PoolPay user profile, the user’s e-mail address, and other basic account information, such as the user’s full name, users’ profile image, age, gender, and birthday, will be shared with PoolPay.

v.	User Content
PoolPay will collect and store the information that the users provide us with when they use our service. Use of service includes creating shared pools, expenses, ledger details, and any other form of communication to utilize the company’s services. This data includes, under its ambit, expense details, bills, receipts, payment information, contributions, images of receipts or bills, or any other relevant payment detail, chats, reminders, memos, notes or any other information users may share to manage their pool. The information shared within groups will not be viewable by users that don’t exist within the group and is only collected and stored by us to improve user service and experience.

However, besides personal groups, PoolPay will provide blog postings, chat servers, message boards, FAQ forums, or other public venues where a user may contact us or other users to discuss their experiences and reviews and provide feedback to the company. Any information submitted or posted to these blogs or other forums can be viewed and made publicly available. Users must maintain cautionary discretion on their behalf of what they post and share on these public forums and servers offered by PoolPay. 

As a result, please be cautious about what you disclose and do not share any information that you wish to remain confidential.

The Company will bear no responsibility for what each user posts on any of these public servers or forums. However, the company may choose to moderate such public forums at its discretion.

vi.	Payment Information
PoolPay will ask the user to provide certain financial information such as debit/credit card number, valid thru date of the card, name on card, billing and contact information, OTPs at payment portal, as well as other account and login information. 

vii.	Miscellaneous Information 
There may exist user surveys and contests to facilitate better company-user interaction, and for that reason, a user may be invited to take part in contests, a survey, or other types of volunteer promotion. In association with one of these campaigns, we may gather contact and/or marketing information. The way we collect information in a given contest or survey may be subject to the nature of the promotion and will bear a disclaimer in the given situations if there may be any differences with regards to data collection from the contents of this privacy policy. In the event there is no change, then no disclaimer shall be provided and the privacy policy shall be applicable as-is.
Additionally, we may collect publicly accessible information about our user bases, such as the full name, email address, phone number, or other demographic data, to assist during support, address fraud or security issues, or customize the user experience, including by displaying advertisements or offers.
We may also request or collect other information, such as feedback, questions, comments, recommendations, or ideas, to provide enhanced and improved service.

Note: If you opt not to provide Personal Information on the Platform, we may be unable to offer you certain, if not all, Platform services. We may make good faith attempts to notify you at the appropriate time. In any situation, we will not be accountable or responsible if we are unable to provide certain services to you due to the users’ failure to provide the required information.

DATA SHARING

PoolPay will NOT SHARE any user data with any Third Party except as stated herein in this privacy statement or without user consent or as under:

•	PoolPay was designed to keep track of, distribute, and pay off costs in a shared ledger known as a Pool. We share some of the users’ personal information with other PoolPay members, such as their names, email addresses, phone number, and user profile picture. Users who have an email address and/or phone number can add other users as a friend and view the users’ names, email addresses, and user profile pictures. Unless a user includes another user in those expenditures or puts them in a group, they won't be able to see the expenditures added with other friends. The onus and caution of who users form a Pool rest solely in the hands of the users and therefore they must filter and select what users they add into their Pool as personal information will be accessible amongst all users that are party to the Pool.

•	To aid and improve our services, we may appoint third-party agents, subsidiaries, affiliates, and/or service providers. Third parties that have access to user personal information on our behalf are contractually obligated to follow our privacy policy and are not permitted to use it for any other reason. 

•	PoolPay reserves the discretionary right to share and disclose user data with law enforcement, governmental authorities, and any other third party to respond to legal processes, court subpoenas, or court directions and in situations of emergency to protect an individual’s safety.

•	In the event of a change of structure of our management due to a merger, acquisition, a business transfer, we may transfer or sell user data to third parties, including but not limited to the personal information and any other information the user may give out to the company.  The responsibility for the user data will be transferred to such a third party and they shall have the rights and duties stated in this Privacy Policy and accordance with relevant legislation.

•	We may disclose non-identifiable information that does not individually identify a user by personal credentials and this includes under its ambit, information shared with third parties for research, marketing, analytics, and/or promotional purposes, as well as to assist us in determining how we might optimize our service further.

•	PoolPay allows access to third-party API services which allows other social media and email apps to gain access to the data users provide to PoolPay. By agreeing to our policy, the user agrees that PoolPay is not responsible or liable for any breach of user privacy by these third-party services and agrees to indemnify PoolPay in the event of an incident.

PoolPay allows API integration solely for the convenience of the user as API integration allows seamless communication between various applications and enables users to get the benefits of multiple cloud-based applications.

If not protested to sharing of user data for the purposes stated above, it will be assumed that consent has been provided. All contracts with third parties that involve the processing of user personal information in the name and on behalf of PoolPay will include a clause ensuring that they provide the same level of personal data protection as PoolPay, as aforementioned in the above clauses.
