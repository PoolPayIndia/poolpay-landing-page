Frequently Asked Questions

PoolPay is a payment platform that aims to make group payments easier, efficient and more transparent. You can pool money into a common digital wallet with your friends, which all of you can collectively use for all their group related payments. Apart from group wallets, PoolPay also provides your with a UPI wallet and a virtual card so you can carry out personal transactions as well.

You can use PoolPay to do a tone of useful things -
1. Create an expense group with your friends which also doubles down as a shared wallet.
2. Not only track, but make and settle group related expenses from your shared digital wallet.
3. Track and make personal payments.
4. Split one time bill payments using our QuickSplit feature.
5. Save money through great offers, cashbacks and rewards through card payments.

While other apps rely on manual entry of expenses and inefficient ways to settle payments, PoolPay allows you to reduce this work by giving you and your friends a shared digital wallet that can be used to make all payments that are common amongst the group members.

The first version of our app is expected to be live by June 2023. Join the waitlist to stay updated.

On launch, you will be able to create chat and expense groups with friends. You will also be able to use the QuickSplit feature to track and reconcile money for one time split of payments. You can check out our roadmap to better understand when all the features will be available on the app.

QuickSplit allows you to track and reconcile money from friends for one time splitting of bills. After creating a QuickSplit for an expense, you will be able to send payments links to the friends involved and keep a track of who has paid and who has not.
